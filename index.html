Overview

I am a fifth-year Electrical Engineering Ph.D. candidate at Columbia University working on research projects centered around edge-cloud computing and integrated sensing and communication for future smart city applications. My research uses millimeter-wave (mmWave) and sub-Terahertz (sub-THz) channel measurements which I collect. I am advised by Prof. Gil Zussman, and I collaborate with Nokia Bell Labs, NOAA-CESSRST, WINLAB, IBM Research, and Center for Smart Streetscapes.

Download CV

Research

Investigating mmWave communications and sensing for Beyond-5G systems, including urban propagation models (MobiHoc '22, Infocom '25), interference mitigation for weather instruments (MobiCom '24), and backscatter localization.
