How it works: A 60 GHz mmWave radar (HMMD S3KM1110) measures the distance to the nearest moving object in front of my door. A “detection” is recorded whenever that distance shifts by more than 3 cm from the previous recorded value. The chart shows every detection for the current day; at midnight ET, the day’s detections are saved to a .txt log file on the sensor.

Why: (1) How often do I actually use my door? (2) When do I use it? (3) Did it open at a time I wasn’t there?
