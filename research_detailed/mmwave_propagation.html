mmWave Propagation Modeling

Developed around-corner and over-the-top channel models for 28 GHz urban scenarios. Conducted extensive measurements in Manhattan, evaluating path loss and angle of arrival (AoA) for multi-user MIMO (MU-MIMO). Achieved 6.4 dB RMSE with the Street-Clutter-NLOS model, outperforming the 3GPP-UMi-NLOS model by 5 dB. Published in MobiHoc '22 and Infocom '25.
