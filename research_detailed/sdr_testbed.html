Software-Defined Radar Testbed

Built an SDR-based radar testbed for multi-target tracking using Joint Probabilistic Data Association (JPDA) filtering. Evaluated over-the-air (OTA) and emulated scenarios, including automotive radar applications at sub-6 GHz and 28 GHz. Demonstrated the testbed's capabilities on the Ben Franklin Bridge scenario, showcasing its potential for rapid prototyping and testing of radar systems.
